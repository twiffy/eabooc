STANDARDIZED TESTING

In this assignment you will learn to interpret standardized test. You will learn about whatever standardized test is most relevant to you and about the controversial new test coming to US schools. This assignment includes online videos that you are expected to watch.

A note about standardized tests: The relevant test for many of you will be the ISTEP or the ECA for which this assignment is specifically tailored. If there is another test that is relevant and it has some sort of interpretation guide, then use that test for this assignment. You may have to modify the assignment a bit to suit you. Look over the interpretation guide and the assignment and if it still does not make sense contact the instructor.

If you do not work with any standardized tests then just complete the assignment with the Indiana ISTEP or GQE. This assignment is really about making sense of a handful of crucial concepts that anybody who has some expertise in assessment should be familiar with, primarily in the context you work in. Feel free to customize this assignment if need be to cover whatever stuff is relevant to interpreting standardized tests in your context.

Please note that your wikis should be stand-alone documents. Do not write them as answers to test questions for the instructor to read. You do not need fancy formatting, but put enough headers and links so that your classmates and instructors can read and compare. You should look and compare but to reiterate you may not directly copy. Keep in mind that each of these key concepts is likely to appear on the final exam, and possibly in more than one format.
