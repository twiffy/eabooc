Assignment 5: Reliability and Absence-of-Bias

In this assignment, you will apply the concepts of Reliability and Bias to the assessments you already created.

There are no right or wrong answers. If you still can't come up with anything to say after reading your classmates entries, then you are free to indicate that in your entries. These are hard questions, and some of you will struggle more than others.  But you will return to these issues throughout the semester.

For further information on bias, it might be helpful to watch this video from the author of our textbook, Popham.

(Based on Popham Chapter 5).
