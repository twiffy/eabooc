VALIDITY

This assignment helps you make sense of the complex concepts of validity by considering the ideas in Chapter 4 relative to your assessments and your role.

1. LESSON & ROLE. Start a new subpage called Validity and summarize your assessment practice (the suite of assessments you have developed) and your institutional role as they relate to validity. Highlight the aspects of your lesson and role that are particularly relevant to these issues. You will certainly have to return to this after you have worked on the lesson for some time.

2. Read/Review Chapter 4. Read the introduction carefully and read the Pondertime questions to shape your assignment. Review the different types of validity evidence.

3. RELEVANCE OF VALIDITY. Summarize the three types of validity evidence and rank them in order of relevance for your role. You need to study them well enough to articulate why the first one is most relevant and why the last one is least relevant. You can borrow ideas from your classmates but not actual text. Then, describe how validity is relevant to teachers, administrators and the public. Your work should address all of the Pondertime questions 1-5.

4. ASSESSMENT REVISIONS. For each of your examples of the following assessments, state which type of validity evidence seems most relevant. Then use the text to suggest how you would examine validity and what you could do to enhance it.

B. Constructed-Response Items
C. Essay Item
D. Performance or Portfolio
