Overview

In this assignment, you will learn the complex concepts of validity by considering concepts related to validity relative to your assessments and your role.  

Review Chapter 4 for this unit.  These are complicated concepts.  Even though Popham does a great job explaining validity, some of you may find it challenging to read this chapter straight through. In particular, validity requires you to think about knowing and learning as distinct from teaching and studying.  This assignment will help you make sense of these ideas.
